Overview of Schluter Balik Funeral Home#

Schluter Balik Funeral Home was founded in 1916 by John and Mary Schluter The Schluters had a passion for helping others, and they dedicated their lives to providing comfort and care to those in need Over the years, their business has grown and expanded, but their commitment to providing compassionate service has stayed the same Today, Schluter Balik Funeral Home is one of the most respected funeral homes in West Michigan, and we are proud to be a part of the community
