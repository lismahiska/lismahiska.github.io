### What is a Paper Airplane?

A paper airplane is a type of model aircraft made from a single sheet of paper or other lightweight material, usually folded into a shape that resembles an airplane. Paper airplanes are typically used as toys, but they can also be used to demonstrate basic principles of aerodynamics.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) :
**Q1: What is a paper airplane?**

**A1:** A paper airplane is a toy aircraft, usually made out of folded paper, that is propelled by a person throwing it.

**Q2: What makes a good paper airplane design?**

**A2:** A good paper airplane design should be balanced, have a good center of gravity, and have enough lift and thrust to stay in the air. It should also be easy to fold and have a streamlined shape.

**Q3: What materials are needed to make a paper airplane?**

**A3:** You will need a piece of paper, scissors, tape, and a pen or pencil.

**Q4: How do you fold a paper airplane?**

**A4:** There are many different ways to fold a paper airplane, but the most basic way is to fold the paper in half lengthwise, then fold the edges in to the center crease. After that, you can make various folds and creases to form the wings and tail of the plane.

**Q5: What is the best way to launch a paper airplane?**

**A5:** The best way to launch a paper airplane is to hold it at the nose, then throw it with a swift, flat motion. Make sure to throw it straight and level, and not too hard.
